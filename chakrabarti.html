WBUR's Megnah Chakrabarti
March 6th, 2018

Radio Boston host Megnah Chakrabarti's career has coincided with one of the most fast-paced periods of change in the journalism industry. She began her career in the years where the post-9/11 world took shape, and has since seen field transform as it adapts to new media. On this episode, we talk with her about making decisions in the modern newsroom, about how she discovered her craft, and how one can begin to make sense of the information landscape.

We would like to extend a thank you Ms. Chakrabarti for sharing her time with us. We encourage you to tune in to 90.9 or go to WBUR's website to find Radio Boston and other programs.
