About Us

The Common Thread Podcast is recorded and produced by 6 undergraduate students at Boston University. Our goal is to seek out and discuss topics important to our community.

We'd like to thank the Howard Thurman Center for supporting this podcast. A special thanks to Ms. Kennedy and Mr. Falci for their support, help and guidance.
